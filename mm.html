Magic Mirror build

Using a Raspberry Pi, two way mirror, and an old Vizio TV, I created an interactive mirror display. The screen displays a calendar, date, time, weather forcast, and the current song playing on the associated Spotify account. The Raspberry Pi grabs data from the GCal API, weather app API, and Spotify broadcast API.
