A brief summary of my work and beliefs

Built my first PC in 8th grade, learned basic hacking and created gaming video production with an online community in high school. Studied computer science in college and continued on to work in software marketing after graduation. Blockchain technology first peaked my interest in 2015-16 when Ethereum first started gaining traction and I read a lot about Eth mining. Later in 2018, I started crypto investing. What excites me most about decentralized finance and blockchain technology is the moral implications and freedom of choice it gives to the world. I am all in for crypto and believe strongly in its utility.

Fresh legs, a vigorous ambition, an unending passion for decentralized finance, and a strong background in social, technical work. I am a valuable asset to any team by bringing energy and enthusiasm. Genuine and down to earth, I can relate to anyone. Looking to drive the future of finance in a moral and decentralized direction
